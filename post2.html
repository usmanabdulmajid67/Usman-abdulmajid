Why I Believe in Sharing Knowledge

June 1, 2025

As both a student and a lecturer, I’ve come to believe that knowledge becomes more valuable when shared. Whether it's through teaching, publishing, or casual discussion, sharing what we know uplifts others—and often deepens our own understanding.

This blog is one way I aim to share my lessons, experiences, and reflections. I hope it becomes a useful resource for anyone eager to learn or teach.
